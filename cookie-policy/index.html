Cookie Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy explains the cookies and similar browser-storage technologies used in connection with oliversconsulting.co.uk.

Our website

We do not use analytics, advertising, behavioural tracking, or profiling cookies.

Your privacy choice

We use a small item of browser storage called localStorage to remember whether you allowed Google Fonts. The stored item is named oc_font_privacy_choice and contains only your choice. It is used so that we do not have to ask you again on every page.

This preference is necessary to remember the privacy choice you made. It remains in your browser until you clear your browser data or change your choice using the Privacy settings button on the website.

Google Fonts

Google Fonts are optional. We do not contact Google to load fonts unless you select Allow Google Fonts. If you allow them, your browser requests the font files from Google. Google may receive technical request data such as your IP address, browser information, and the page making the request.

We do not use Google Fonts for analytics or advertising. You can withdraw your choice at any time using the Privacy settings button. The page will reload and Google Fonts will no longer be requested.

Cookies when you book a call

If you follow the link to book a call, you are taken to a scheduling page provided by Cal.eu. That separate website may set cookies needed to run the booking. Its own cookie and privacy terms apply. Those cookies are only set if you choose to visit the booking page.

Managing browser storage

You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Clearing the site’s stored data will reset your Google Fonts choice and the privacy banner will appear again.
