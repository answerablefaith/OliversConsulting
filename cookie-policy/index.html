Cookie Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy explains the use of cookies in connection with oliversconsulting.co.uk.

Cookies on this website

My website is a static information page. It does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies, and it does not build a profile of your visit. The site sets no cookies.

Cookies when you book a call

If you follow the link to book a call, you are taken to a scheduling page provided by Cal.com. That page sets its own cookies needed to run the booking, and they are governed by Cal.com’s own cookie and privacy terms, not this policy. These cookies are only set if you choose to use the booking link.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Guidance for each browser is available at aboutcookies.org.
